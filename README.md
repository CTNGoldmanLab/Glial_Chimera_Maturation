# Glial_Chimera_Maturation
scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, and CUT&Tag analysis of ESC/iPSCs, their differentiated GPCs, and their post-transplantation derivatives.

[Link to Preprint: Charting the transition from in vitro gliogenesis to the in vivo maturation of transplanted human glial progenitor cells](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.04.22.649367v1 "Link to Preprint")
